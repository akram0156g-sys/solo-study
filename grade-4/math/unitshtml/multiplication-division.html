Lesson 3 — Division (No Remainder)

Flashcards: Division (No Remainder)

Click Next Step to work through the long division, step by step.

84 ÷ 4 = ?
Step 1 — How many times does 4 go into 8? → 2. Write 2 above.
Step 2 — 2 × 4 = 8. Subtract: 8 − 8 = 0. Bring down the 4.
Step 3 — How many times does 4 go into 4? → 1. Write 1 above.
✅ 84 ÷ 4 = 21
126 ÷ 6 = ?
Step 1 — How many times does 6 go into 12? → 2. Write 2 above.
Step 2 — 2 × 6 = 12. Subtract: 12 − 12 = 0. Bring down the 6.
Step 3 — How many times does 6 go into 6? → 1. Write 1 above.
✅ 126 ÷ 6 = 21
252 ÷ 7 = ?
Step 1 — How many times does 7 go into 25? → 3 (3 × 7 = 21). Write 3 above.
Step 2 — Subtract: 25 − 21 = 4. Bring down the 2 → working number is 42.
Step 3 — How many times does 7 go into 42? → 6. Write 6 above.
✅ 252 ÷ 7 = 36
504 ÷ 8 = ?
Step 1 — How many times does 8 go into 50? → 6 (6 × 8 = 48). Write 6 above.
Step 2 — Subtract: 50 − 48 = 2. Bring down the 4 → working number is 24.
Step 3 — How many times does 8 go into 24? → 3. Write 3 above.
✅ 504 ÷ 8 = 63
945 ÷ 9 = ?
Step 1 — How many times does 9 go into 9? → 1. Write 1 above.
Step 2 — 1 × 9 = 9. Subtract: 9 − 9 = 0. Bring down 4. 9 goes into 4 → 0 times. Bring down 5 → working number is 45.
Step 3 — How many times does 9 go into 45? → 5. Write 05 above.
✅ 945 ÷ 9 = 105

Lesson 4 — Division with Remainders

Flashcards: Division with Remainders

Click Next Step to work through the problem step by step.

17 ÷ 5 = ?
Step 1 — How many times does 5 go into 17 without going over? → 3 (3 × 5 = 15).
Step 2 — Multiply: 3 × 5 = 15. Subtract: 17 − 15 = 2.
Step 3 — 2 is less than 5, so we can't divide further. The remainder is 2.
✅ 17 ÷ 5 = 3 R 2
25 ÷ 4 = ?
Step 1 — How many times does 4 go into 25 without going over? → 6 (6 × 4 = 24).
Step 2 — Multiply: 6 × 4 = 24. Subtract: 25 − 24 = 1.
Step 3 — 1 is less than 4, so we stop. The remainder is 1.
✅ 25 ÷ 4 = 6 R 1
